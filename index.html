Our Golf Course

Kalinowe Pola Golf Course stretches over 60 hectares of meticulously maintained greens and fairways. With 18 challenging holes, strategic bunkers, and breathtaking natural views, our course is designed to provide an exceptional golfing experience for both beginners and seasoned professionals alike.

Accommodation

Comfort Rooms

Experience comfort and convenience in our spacious rooms, perfectly designed for players who want to stay just steps away from the green. Each room offers a serene environment to relax after a day on the course.

Luxury Suites

Indulge in unparalleled luxury with our exquisite suites. Ideal for a relaxing getaway amidst nature, our suites provide full on-site service, premium amenities, and stunning views for an unforgettable experience.

Golf Academy

Our Golf Academy offers comprehensive lessons tailored for all skill levels, from absolute beginners to advanced players looking to refine their game. All lessons are taught by certified PGA professionals dedicated to helping you achieve your golfing goals.
